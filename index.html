How it works

Your search runs live against the US federal recall APIs and against local copies of the Canadian and UK registries that refresh once a day. Every result links straight to the official government record — we add the cross-database search, not the data.

The verdict distinguishes recent recalls (newest record within 18 months), past recalls, and no records found. Recalls almost always target specific lots, date codes, or model numbers, so always confirm your exact product against the linked official notice.
